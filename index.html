Lionel Andres Messi

Lionel Messi

An Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward for and captains both Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and the Argentina national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all-time, Messi has won a record eight Ballon d'Or awards and a record six European Golden Shoes, and in 2020 he was named to the Ballon d'Or Dream Team.

Career

Lionel Messi

Lionel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who has played for three clubs in his 19-year-long professional career He started his youth career at Grandoli and Newell’s Old Boys before joining Barcelona in 2000. Messi played for Barcelona from 2004 to 2021, where he won a club-record 34 trophies, including ten La Liga titles, seven Copa del Rey titles, and the UEFA Champions League four times. In 2021, he joined Paris Saint-Germain and played for them until 2023 2. Currently, he is playing for Inter Miami in Major League Soccer. Messi has also represented Argentina in international football. He has scored 106 goals in 180 appearances for the national team . Messi won the 2021 Copa América and the 2022 FIFA World Cup with Argentina. Messi has received a total of 8 Ballon d’Or awards, the most for any football player, as well as the 2009 FIFA World Player of the Year, and the 2019 and 2022 The Best FIFA Men’s Player. Messi holds the record for the most goals in La Liga (474), the Supercopa de España (14), the UEFA Super Cup (3) and is the player with the most official recorded assists in football history (357). Messi is the first and only player in history to win five and six European Golden Shoes . Messi has scored 821 goals for club and country throughout his professional career. Messi is the most decorated footballer of all time having won a total of 44 collective trophies
